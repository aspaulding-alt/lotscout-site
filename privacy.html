Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. We keep it simple and honest.

1. Who We Are

The Lot Scout is operated by Spaulding Asset Solutions, LLC dba Not Just Auctions, based in Moore, Oklahoma. We provide an automated auction search and alert service at thelotscout.com.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: aspaulding@notjustauctions.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Email address — when you sign up for our waitlist or create an account
  • Keywords — the search terms you save to your account
  • Subscription information — your plan tier and billing status (payment details are handled by Stripe and never stored on our servers)
  • Usage data — how you interact with the site, which features you use
  • Device and browser information — standard web analytics data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you email alerts when listings matching your keywords are found
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Improve the service and fix issues
  • Send you product updates and announcements (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Ever.

4. Email Communications

When you sign up, you'll receive:

  • Alert emails — sent when new listings matching your keywords are found. These are the core service and are sent up to 3 times daily.
  • Transactional emails — account confirmations, password resets, billing receipts.
  • Product updates — occasional news about new features and sites added. You can unsubscribe from these at any time.

Every marketing email includes an unsubscribe link. We comply with the CAN-SPAM Act.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies necessary to operate the service — primarily to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising trackers or sell data to ad networks.

We may use basic analytics (such as page view counts) to understand how the site is used. This data is aggregated and not tied to individual users.

6. Third Party Services

We use the following third party services:

  • Mailchimp — email list management and marketing emails. Mailchimp Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — payment processing. Your card details go directly to Stripe and are never stored on our servers. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Render — website hosting

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Our site runs on HTTPS, passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text, and we limit access to personal data to only what is necessary to operate the service.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you discover a security issue, please contact us immediately at aspaulding@notjustauctions.com.

8. Data Retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or accounting purposes.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Export your saved keywords

To exercise any of these rights, email us at aspaulding@notjustauctions.com.

10. Children's Privacy

The Lot Scout is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice on the site. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last updated.
